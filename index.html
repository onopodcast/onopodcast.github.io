Hello! I'm Roy Harmon, a marketer (not a web designer, as you can see). I've seen a lot of people in my LinkedIn feed looking for marketing jobs, so I thought:

"Hey, why not start a podcast to showcase the marketing skills of #jobseekers on LinkedIn?!"

Well, I couldn't think of any reason not to, so wouldn't you know it, that's just what I've done. Or at least I'm trying to. I'm calling it the #ONO Podcast. (For the uninitiated, "ONO" stands for Open to New Opportunities."

But before we get to that part, I want to apologize for the color scheme. I pulled it from a Saved by the Bell logo I found on Google Images and I just couldn't convince myself not to use it.
